About Me

A Doctor Who Knows What it Means to Move

I'm Dr. Chinedum Arize — a General Practitioner working within the NHS in London, with a specialist focus on sports injuries and musculoskeletal health. My philosophy is simple: the human body is built to move, and movement is the most powerful medicine we have.

My clinical background spans acute GP care, preventative medicine, and sports injury assessment. I work with patients ranging from weekend warriors to competitive athletes, helping them recover faster and get back to doing what they love.

Outside the clinic, I practice what I preach — I've completed multiple marathons and am a consistent runner and fitness advocate. I understand the frustration of injury because I've lived it, and I know the road back.
